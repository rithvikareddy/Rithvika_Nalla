How I owned the AI product roadmap that saved Delta $1M in pilot reserve costs
AI / ML Product Product Owner AWS SageMaker Crew Operations A/B Testing
The Problem

Delta's crew ops team had no reliable way to predict next-day pilot reserve demand. Schedulers defaulted to conservative over-allocation, triggering unnecessary Green Slip premium activations.

With next-day reserve coverage sitting at just 41%, the cost exposure was significant enough to warrant an AI-driven solution — but only if the model could meet FAA regulatory requirements and collective bargaining constraints simultaneously.

Key Constraints

The solution had to operate within FAA regulations governing pilot scheduling and adhere strictly to collective bargaining agreement constraints — leaving no room for model errors that violated labor rules.

Latency was non-negotiable: demand forecasting outputs needed to be delivered under 300ms to support real-time crew scheduling decisions. Any regression in model accuracy during iterative updates required zero-downtime rollback protocols.
